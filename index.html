Hidden Gems of Austria

Discover Austria beyond the usual tourist spots

Gosausee

Nestled in Upper Austria near Hallstatt, Gosausee offers breathtaking alpine scenery, hiking trails, and a serene lake reflecting the Dachstein mountains.

Heiligenblut

This picturesque village at the foot of Grossglockner is a peaceful base for alpine adventures and offers one of Austria’s most iconic church views.

Kölnbreinsperre Dam

Located in Carinthia, this high alpine dam offers dramatic views, a skywalk platform, and access to remote hiking routes in the Malta Valley.
